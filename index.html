Winning Percentage Formula

The winning percentage is calculated using this simple formula:

Winning Percentage = (Wins / Total Games) × 100

Where Total Games = Wins + Losses

How to Calculate Winning Percentage

Calculating your team's winning percentage is simple:

  1. Count the total number of games played (wins + losses)
  2. Divide the number of wins by the total games
  3. Multiply by 100 to get the percentage

Example Calculation

If your team has:

  • Wins: 15
  • Losses: 5
  • Total Games: 20

Winning Percentage = (15 / 20) × 100 = 75%

Tips for Using the Calculator

  • Enter whole numbers only (no decimals)
  • Make sure both wins and losses are entered
  • The result will be shown as a percentage
